Have you considered visiting London, England for your next trip? If yes, then you came in the right place!

London is the capital city of England, and the United Kingdom. This city is the Home of the Royals. As we all know, London is known for its famous Big Ben, Buckingham Palace and the Tower Bridge, but this city has so much more to offer!

Here are the top 5 must visit places in London.

1. London eye

London Eye,is one of the famous landmarks in the UK. This Millenium Wheel is also one of the reasons why London Skyline is is the best at night. If you would like to see the 360° views of the City, this will be the best bet.

2. Buckingham Palace

The Buckingham Palace, located in the city of Westminster. is the home of Queen Elizabeth II. We will often see Royal Guards around this area.

3. Tower Bridge

THIS IS NOT THE LONDON BRIDGE! People often call it by the wrong name, but this is called the Tower Bridge. This is the iconic London's landmark and visitors or tourist will always have a picture in this location, so don't miss it!

4. Westminster Abbey

Westminster Abbey, "A Royal Church", is the site of ceremonies. This is where 17 Royal Weddings had taken place.

5. Big Ben

Big Ben is the Great Big Bell in London. Unfortunately, Big Ben is under construction since 2017, so you won't be able to see it since it is covered with scaffolding during this time. Hopefully the construction will be done by 2022.
